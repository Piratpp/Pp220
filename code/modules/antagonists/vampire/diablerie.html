/datum/diablerie"Diablerie" thing is entirely World of Darkness reference. In simple terms, 'diablerie' is the act of a vampire killing another vampire by draining all of their blood, thereby taking their soul and becoming more powerful. The word "diablerie" comes from French "diable" + "erie", which means something like "devilish practice"
