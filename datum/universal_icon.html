universal_icon
/datum/universal_icon

This is intended to replace /icon, allowing rustlib to generate icons much faster than DM can at scale. Construct these with the uni_icon() proc, in the same manner as BYOND's icon() proc. Additionally supports a number of transform procs (lowercase, rather than BYOND's uppercase) such as Crop, Scale, and Blend (as blend_icon/blend_color).
